The pipeline

From topic to sent campaign in six steps

You provide a topic. The system handles everything else — writing, segmenting, reviewing, sending, and learning.

1
You type a topic
Something like "AI tools every creative agency should use in 2026." That's the only input required. No briefing docs, no templates to fill out.
2
AI writes the blog post
Claude (Anthropic's AI model) drafts a 400–600 word article with a compelling headline, clear structure, and an SEO meta description. It already knows what topics and angles have performed well from previous campaigns.
→ Publish-ready blog draft
3
AI writes three different newsletter versions
The same blog becomes three separate emails — one for agency owners (ROI-focused, executive tone), one for freelance creatives (casual, practical), and one for marketing managers (data-forward, team-oriented). Each has a tailored subject line, preview text, and call to action.
→ 3 audience-targeted emails
4
You review and approve
The draft lands in the dashboard marked Pending Approval. You read the blog and the three newsletters. If they look good, you click Approve & Send. Nothing goes out without a human sign-off.
5
Emails are sent to the right audience
Each newsletter version is delivered to its matching audience segment via the Resend email platform. Agency owners get the agency owner version. Freelancers get theirs. Contacts are tracked in HubSpot.
→ Campaign sent & logged
6
AI reports on performance and suggests what's next
After sending, the system generates a plain-English performance summary: which audience opened and clicked most, any warning signs, and 2–3 specific things to do differently next time. It also suggests three topics for your next campaign, informed by what has worked historically.
→ AI performance report + next topic ideas

Design decisions

Three things that make this different

Most AI content tools generate and forget. This one is built to learn and improve with every send.

01
Emails actually send
Most demos simulate email delivery. This pipeline connects to Resend and delivers real HTML emails to real inboxes — free tier, no credit card required. You see actual delivery confirmations, not mock logs.
02
It learns from every campaign
After each send, the system records which subject lines, topics, and calls-to-action drove the highest click rates. The next time it writes a newsletter, those winning patterns are already baked into its instructions.
03
Humans stay in control
Content is drafted and saved first. A human reviews it in the dashboard, then clicks Approve & Send. Nothing reaches a real inbox without explicit sign-off — the system assists, it doesn't replace judgment.
